2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted third parties only as necessary to operate our business:

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Estimate requests that do not convert to active clients are deleted within 12 months. You may request earlier deletion at any time by contacting us.
