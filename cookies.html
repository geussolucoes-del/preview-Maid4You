1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners. Cookies do not contain personally identifiable information by themselves, but they can be combined with other data to identify you.

2. How We Use Cookies

The Maid4You Cleaning Services website uses a minimal set of cookies to ensure the site functions correctly and to understand how visitors use it. We do not use cookies for advertising, behavioral tracking, or selling data to third parties.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Performance / Analytics Anonymous data about page visits and interactions, used to improve user experience. Only enabled with your consent. Up to 13 months Yes (e.g. Google Analytics)

We do not use marketing, advertising, or social media tracking cookies.

4. Third-Party Cookies

If analytics are enabled, we use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. Google may set cookies on your device to measure site activity. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. You can learn more about Google’s data practices at policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Your Cookie Choices

You have the right to accept or reject non-essential cookies. You can control cookie settings in the following ways:

  • Browser settings — Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. Note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
  • Google Analytics opt-out — You can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to prevent your data from being used.
  • Contact us — If you have specific concerns, contact us at hello@maid4you.com and we will assist you.
